Chicken Broccoli and Ziti

Description

This is a popular Italian pasta dish called chicken broccoli and ziti!This dish consists of a pasta of your liking, chicken, and broccoli. This popular dish is often topped off with creamy alfredo sauce and parmasean cheese. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Ziti
  • Creamy Alfredo sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Cook chicken
  3. Steam broccoli
  4. Add desired toppings
  5. Pour a glass of wine and enjoy a taste of Italy with your family
