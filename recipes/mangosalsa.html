Mango Salsa

Description

Mango salsa is one of the greatest combinations of food in the world. Sweet and succulent mangos, a mild kick from the spicy japalenos, extra depth with the onions, and finally, lemon juice to top it all off

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Mangos
  • 1/4 Onion
  • 1 Japaleno
  • 1-2 lemons and/or limes juiced
  • Salt, pepper, and a dash of cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the mangos, japaleno, and onions and put them into a bowl
  2. Juice your lemons and limes into the bowl
  3. Finally, season and mix all together. Enjoy!
