Tilapia Tacos

Description

Tilapia tacos, one of the world's most delicious... and photogenic foods. Tilapia is a type of fish with a similar texture to salmon. Tilapia tacos taste best when topped off with an array of vegetables, and my favorite, mango salsa!If you're interested in my mango salsa recipe I've included a link to it right here

Ingredients

  • 3 tilapia filets
  • Tortillas
  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Mango Salsa (highly recommended)

Steps

  1. Fry tilapia filets for 2 minutes each side
  2. Heat up tortillas for a few minutes in the oven until warm and soft
  3. Add vegetables and toppings of your choice
  4. Finally, add mango salsa! Last chance if you want the recipe you can find it here.
